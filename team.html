MindFit For All is a not-for-profit organisation, founded by Alyssa Gunn and Simon Desborough.

Alyssa Gunn

Alyssa is a qualified teacher and SENDCo , and holds a diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy. She is experienced in safeguarding as well as special educational needs and disabilities, and has worked closely with many vulnerable children and families. Alyssa is also a marathon runner, completing the London Marathon in 2018 and 2019 and is currently training for a triathlon.

Simon Desborough

Simon is a qualified personal trainer with years of experience and his own personal training/bootcamp business. He provides PT and bootcamp sessions for adults and children and runs weekly sessions in local schools and pre-schools. Simon coaches and trains people with a range of abilities, from beginners to experienced athletes.

Peter Abrams

Peter is a is a TED speaker, Musician & Best-Selling author who broke into the entertainment industry under the mentorship of Russell Brand. Alongside his creative pursuits, Peter is also an FA Level 2 Football Coach and is currently working towards his UEFA license. Peter has spoken in over 250 schools and youth organisations in order to promote and support young people with their mental health.

Jess Adderley

Jess holds a BSC in Sports Coaching Science and Disability Sport. She works with both able bodied participants, as well as participants with disabilities. Working and volunteering within the disability sports sector to aid and inspire athletes of all ages and abilities to participate within sport and/or physical activity, has enabled Jess to build a solid foundation of skills in both inclusive and adapted sports.
